Frequently asked questions

What people often ask me

Have another question? Message me on WhatsApp and I'll reply personally.

What is the first consultation like?
It's an evaluation session where I listen to your case, we review your habits, what you've tried, and what your body needs. You leave with a clear initial plan tailored to you.
Do you offer online consultations or only in-person?
Both. If you're in Panama City, you can come to the in-person consultation at Clínica Mollin. If you're elsewhere, we schedule via video call with the same follow-up.
Will I have to follow a strict diet?
No. My approach is sustainable nutrition: no eliminating food groups, no impossible rules. We work with your routine, your culture, and what you can actually maintain.
How long does the process take?
It depends on your case and goal. Most people achieve lasting changes in 3 to 6 months of follow-up, with sessions every few weeks.
Do you treat specific conditions (insulin resistance, PCOS, gastritis, etc.)?
Yes. I work with conditions like insulin resistance, PCOS, hypothyroidism, gastritis, irritable bowel and more. Each plan is designed based on your diagnosis and needs.
Do you offer consultations in English?
Yes, sessions can be in Spanish or English. Just let me know when scheduling and we'll coordinate in your preferred language.
